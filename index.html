Who I Am

I am a cybersecurity practitioner from Multan, Pakistan, currently completing a BS in Cybersecurity at Air University. My work sits across SOC operations, threat intelligence, OSINT investigations, and malware analysis — with a growing focus on digital risk monitoring and adversary profiling.

I got into cybersecurity the way most people do — curiosity, then obsession. I started competing in CTFs during my first year, built a home lab before I finished my second semester, and spent the time most students spend on assignments building Splunk dashboards and reverse-engineering malware samples instead.

First in my village to pursue higher education. District-level racing competitor (3rd place). I bring the same competitive drive to cybersecurity.
